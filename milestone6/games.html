BlackJack

A card game where players try to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over, while beating the dealer’s hand.

Texas Hold 'em

A type of poker where players are dealt two cards and combine them with five shared cards to make the best possible hand.

Slot Machines

A machine-based game where players spin reels with symbols, aiming to match combinations that result in payouts.

Craps

A dice game where players bet on the outcome of two dice. The shooter rolls to hit a “point” before rolling a seven, while other players can place various bets on the results.

Roulette

A game where players bet on where a ball will land on a spinning wheel of numbered slots. Bets can be on numbers, colors, or other combinations, and payouts vary by odds.

Baccarat

Baccarat is a simple card game where players bet on the “Player,” “Banker,” or a tie. The goal is to have a hand value closest to 9. Cards are dealt according to set rules, and the highest hand wins.
